ChatGPT Efficiency Evaluation

Research Approach

For this project, I developed a backend using Node.js and MongoDB to integrate with the ChatGPT API. The goal was to evaluate ChatGPT's efficiency in responding to domain-specific questions while ensuring accuracy and reliability.

Dataset Overview

The dataset included questions from three domains:

  • History: Testing knowledge of historical events, timelines, and figures
  • Social Science: Analyzing social behavior, societal trends, and human interactions
  • Computer Security: Addressing security concepts, vulnerabilities, and best practices
